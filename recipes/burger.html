Burger is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. The patties are often served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon.

INGREDIENTS TO MAKE BURGERS :

  • burger buns
  • cheese slice
  • ground beef
  • tomatoes
  • onions , pickles, lettuce
  • mayonnaise

Steps to prepare burgers :

  1. Shape ground beef into patties, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry patties until cooked through.
  3. Toast buns and add condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo).
  4. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and the patty.
