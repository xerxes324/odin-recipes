Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. In Italian cuisine it is made of stacked layers of pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings and spices.

INGREDIENTS TO MAKE LASAGNA :

  • Pasta sheets
  • mozzarella cheese
  • ground beef 1kg
  • ricotta cheese
  • marinara sauce
  • salt and pepper

Steps to prepare lasagna :

  1. Cook pasta sheets, drain, and set aside.
  2. Brown your ground beef, add marinara sauce, and simmer.
  3. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 30-40 minutes until bubbly.
